What is Full Stack Development?

Full stack development is the practice of building both the client-side (frontend) and server-side (backend) of a web application. A full stack developer understands how the browser renders HTML/CSS/JS, how APIs expose business logic, and how databases persist and retrieve data.

Frontend

  • HTML for semantic structure and accessibility.
  • CSS for layout, typography, and responsive design.
  • JavaScript for interactivity and client-side logic.

Backend

  • Runtime & Frameworks (e.g., Node.js & Express) to serve requests and implement business logic.
  • APIs (REST/JSON) to communicate between client and server.
  • Databases (SQL or NoSQL) to store and query data efficiently.
